Play Station 653

Full steam ahead for the perfect play date.

An interactive play center where children can journey from station to station exploring their imagination. Our indoor playground is designed as a whimsical train station, where each “stop” is a new themed destination for little explorers. Created for children ages six months to seven years, our play space encourages imaginative play, creativity, and social interaction through thoughtfully designed, hands-on environments. Whether it’s pretending to be a chef at the diner or a guest at the circus, kids and caregivers alike will enjoy a safe, engaging space to play, learn, and grow—together.
